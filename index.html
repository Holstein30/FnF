Rules

Games will be played in a Battle Royale Total of 2 format

A Total of 2 is a Battle Royale match that is won by having the most kills at the end of playing 2 maps.

In a Battle Royale game mode match the hosting team will start a duo or squads match based on the teams size, a Duo's for 1v1 and Squads for 2v2. It is NOT based on who last the longest, whichever team has the most kills at the end of the game wins.

In the event of a tie a tie breaker map will be played until there is a clear winner.

Sabotage/Disqualification

Teams may work together when playing. They also may kill steal, loot steal and follow each other around but are not allowed to Sabotage as described below:

Teams are not allowed to destroy chests the opposing team are actively going for or build a wall that intentionally blocks your opponent from getting loot or a kill.

Intentionally blocking your opponent's shots/travel or loot acquisition when not on a structure built by you or your team is a forfeitable offense if the act is found to be intentional and has a drastic effect on the outcome of the match it is at the admins discretion to forfeit the team.

You can build platforms or walls on your OWN ramps after 3 builds high, if your opponent decides to use your ramp they risk being blocked without forfeit.

Members are not allowed to cause the death, injury, or sabotage of any of their cooperative opponent(s) (co-ponent). A word of advice would be to simply stay away from your opponents.

This can include but is not limited to intentionally destroying/damaging their built structures/cover, making them fall, purposely hitting them with boogie bombs, impulse grenades, or to have caused an interruption of healing/revival.

Shooting at or around your opponent in a manor to draw attention to your opponent will result in a forfeit. This is at the admins discretion if it has a drastic effect on the outcome of the match. Proof of an opponent obviously sabotaging the other team in the defined cases above as well as any actions otherwise directly affecting the outcome of the game in an unsportsmanlike manner can result in a forfeit at admin's discretion. A word of advice would be to simply stay away from your opponents.

We must have clear valid video proof that your opponent sabotaged your match to warrant a forfeit or replay if the proof is not clear we can not make the call properly. Please note all decisions are the admins discretion.

In order for sabotage to occur you would have to have a chance to win. For instance if you are are down 5 kills and there are 4 players remaining you could not possibly win therefore sabotage can not occur.
